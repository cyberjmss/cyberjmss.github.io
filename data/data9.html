This graph depicts the total weekly personal income by sex. The graph shows a massive association with high/low income with each gender group, with women populating the no-income end, and men dominating the high-income end. Among the low-income ranges, a massive amount of women outnumbers all men labourers. This is mostly because of a legacy “housewife” belief which made it socially awkward for women to pursue tertiary education for better income, and women who are actively seeking for work can only find low-income jobs without tertiary education in their resume.
