This graph displays how family households earn significantly more than non-family households, with significantly more family households earning more than 650 dollars weekly and onwards. This is likely because non-family households typically only have one source of income, while family households benefit from multiple sources of income, like partners, children and parents contributing financially. Additionally, family households often have much higher expenses than non-family households as having children significantly increases their expenses like electricity, food and water, hence making it necessary to have a higher income for a family household to be sustainable.
