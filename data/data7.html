This chart displays the amount of people working in a specific industry depending on their sex. Interestingly, males dominate the industry of jobs such as managers, technicians and trade workers, machinery operators and labourers. Meanwhile, women dominate the industry of jobs such as community and personal service workers, clerical and administrative workers, sales workers and professionals. This imbalance may be influenced by historical roles and societal expectations/stereotypes, where men have traditionally taken on more physical and technical jobs, while women have been associated with jobs that involve caretaking and administrative skills. With this data, we can encourage gender norms to shift and encourage women to pursue jobs in male dominated industries and vice versa, which would lead to a more balanced workplace in the future for all involved.
